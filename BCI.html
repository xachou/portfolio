My role

This project is the curriculum project of user interface interaction design ,I was assigned the specific material of the appreciation of Dunhuang art seris and interation chanel - Brain Computer Interaction (BCI). I begin with the framing of primary interview questions, then I concluded and reviewed the material with desk study, designed the final low-fi interface and interaction systerm.

Goal

The insiders strongly recommended me not try to interpret the lessons in the Buddhist Sutras. Since it might cause misleading either for my misinterpretation or for misunderstanding by the visitors. After thorough discussion with the insiders and my supervisor, I decided to put emphasis on popularizing Buddhist culture in experimental interactive method( Brain Computer Interaction ).

Brain Computer Interaction

Every significantly novel and advanced technology looks like magic in the first time. The discover of brian wave by Germany Scientist Hansberg in late 19th Century opened a new door for people to explore the mysteries right in our own bodies. According to the research, the nontrauma equipment can now detect 4 types of wave when brain saty in different activities. Depart from the δ wave and Θ wave which occurred when people are sleeping, we can get two signal from people when they are awake. The α, when people are relaxed; the β wave when people are nervous or highly focus on sth.

Challenges

The limited detected signal from non-intrusive Electroencephalography (EGG) helmet means the users will have less approaches to operate the screen and to interact with the abundant information behind the digital frescoes.

Design The Interaction Mechanism

The interaction technology can be divdided into two steps, clikc and launch. With the specifcation of Buddhism culture and the limitation of EGG helmet hardware, it is necessary to sepecrate these to actions into two simple step – navigating and activating.

The navigating hardware will help the user to locate and choose which object is his or her target easily

The BCI device is supposed to play as a launch installation, imitating meditating in the Buddhism culture

PartA -- Innovate the Device

Because people has little access with the Brain Computer Interaction, this part is supposed to help the users to get familiar with the divce and a first fresh quick glance at how to use brain wave to interact with the virtual stuff.

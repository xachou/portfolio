CONTEXT
At a Glance

Daimler China invited Service Design Institution (SDI), Tsinghua University to launch a workshop for them and help them to find a solution which can ease staff's pressure in the work. We need to develop a design thinking tools which focus on these topic and friendly for non-design background participants.

RESEARCH & DRAFT
Tool-Boxes

I have made thorough desk study and researched hundreds Design Thinking Tools from books and paper to develop optimum ones.


GUIDE HANDBOOK
Fun Work

I designed two items for this workshop. One is the handbook which give the participants to get a realization of which step they are at, what to do next, how to find pain points and evaluate their ideas. One is the blank form and diagram.

Empathy

Interview their colleague and practice the very beginning of user research with the help of tips. Use Persona to create a specific person fit Daimler's working climate. Use the journey map to find existing and potential painpoints





Evaluation

Use the Profit- Cost coordinate to evaluate which pain points deserve to be solved.

IDEATE

Brain storm and evaluate their solutions through different perspectives, HR cost, time cost, urgent level, practical difficulties and etc.
Then the participants choose several (5, 6) reference to evaluate the feasibility of their idea and solutions.


PROTOTYPE

Draw the story board to quick describe the problem and problem solving scenario. Then use the role playing to simulate and prototype this solution.


PROTOTYPE

Share solutions by presentation and discuss with other group.

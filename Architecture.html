Competition Ermei Dinning Hall Architecture

Ermei is the most popular dinning hall in Shanghai University(SHU). A large number of students need to take breakfast after morning running in the nearby playground.Meanwhile, students always complain about they cannot find a place to social and hold activities. Poly is aim to build a tiny landmark for SHU and satisfy students needs.

My Role

As the leader of this team, we won the third prize as the only team whose background is not architecture, and as the only team who has finished task -- prototype 1:30 hand-made model in two weeks.

Effect Pictures

Bubble map has presented a better visualization of data, but it is still hard to display the figures of the aged trees like growth condition, endangered degree, protect status and so forth.


New Ermei is evoluted into a two-floor cafateria with self-study area, lecture space and dinning area.



Students and faculty will be able to chat, hold lectures and activities in this open space, the second floor is a relatively closed space where students can have dinner and self-study.
